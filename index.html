Who we are

A tool and die shop first. A production partner for decades.

B.M.L. Tool and Manufacturing was founded in 1968 in the Bridgeport, Connecticut area and is now in its third generation of family ownership. We take pride in delivering consistent, high-quality service to our customers, some of whom we have worked with for decades.

Without a reliable manufacturing partner, projects stall, deadlines slip, and budgets overrun. With more than five decades of tool and die, manufacturing, and engineering experience, BML helps customers hit their goals through certified processes and meticulous attention to detail.

How we work

Production at your pace. Our process scales with it.

Whether you need a rapid prototype, a pilot run, or a long-term program with steady supply, our systems flex to meet the demand.

Consultation & quoting

We review your prints, materials, volumes, and timeline in detail to determine the most efficient manufacturing approach and confirm we are the right partner for the job.

Engineering & tooling

Our in-house engineering team handles solid-modeling CAD design, die and fixture build, and prototyping, taking product development from sketches through to production-ready tooling.

Production & inspection

Parts run on flexible schedules with statistical process control and CMM inspection throughout, so every shipment arrives on time and to your exact design tolerances.
