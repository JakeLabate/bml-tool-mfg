Our Company

Founded in 1968 in the Bridgeport, Connecticut area, B.M.L. Tool and Manufacturing is now in its third generation of family ownership.

Our story

Fifty-plus years of tool and die, manufacturing, and engineering

B.M.L. Tool and Manufacturing is an ISO 9001:2015 certified, full-service contract manufacturer and developer of precision metal components and assemblies, operating today from Monroe, Connecticut.

We began as a tool and die shop in the Bridgeport area in 1968. Three generations of family ownership later, that heritage still shapes how we work: tooling is designed and built in-house, the tool room supports the press room, and engineering sits steps from production. It is why we can respond quickly when a customer needs a new part, a repaired die, or a smarter process.

We pride ourselves on providing consistent, high-quality service to our customers, some of whom we have worked with for decades. BML is a veteran-owned small business with a team of about 60 skilled tradespeople and support staff.

  • Efficient

    Tight integration between departments lets us respond quickly to new opportunities and act fast when a process improvement is identified.

  • Deeply committed

    Much of our management and workforce has been with the company for decades and shares a vested interest in the quality of every part that ships.

  • Highly skilled

    Our toolmakers, CNC programmers, and press operators are masters of their trades and produce consistent, quality work.

Experience and expertise

Cross-industry range, single-shop accountability

With more than five decades of tool and die, manufacturing, and engineering experience, we have supplied parts, tooling, and fixtures to many industries, including medical, electrical, defense, aerospace, law enforcement, food service, heavy manufacturing, and recreation.

That range is not accidental. The disciplines that hold up in defense and medical work, documented processes, verified tooling, and measured results, carry over to every other job in the shop.
